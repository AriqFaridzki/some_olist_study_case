#TLDR
Olist in an business to business to Consumer type business (B2B2C)

#About Olist
Olist is just a big department store that have various items (like Matahari Mall clothing store). the product is provided by seller that have agreement with olist. Olist is a 3rd party that provide brand image and easiness.

The interesting about olist is that, seller does not have to handle various setup with other marketplace, this is done by olist with a lot of partner.
